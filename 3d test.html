About Mars

Geography

Mars features some of the most impressive geological features in our solar system, including Olympus Mons, the largest volcano, and Valles Marineris, the largest canyon.

Atmosphere

The Martian atmosphere is primarily composed of carbon dioxide (95.3%), nitrogen (2.7%), and argon (1.6%). The atmospheric pressure is less than 1% of Earth's.

Water on Mars

Evidence suggests that liquid water once flowed on Mars's surface. Today, water exists primarily as ice in the polar ice caps and beneath the surface.

Mars Exploration

Current Missions

Several active missions are currently exploring Mars, including NASA's Perseverance rover and the Ingenuity helicopter.

Future Plans

Upcoming missions aim to return Martian samples to Earth and establish human presence on the Red Planet.

Scientific Discoveries

Recent discoveries include evidence of ancient microbial life and complex organic molecules in Martian soil.
